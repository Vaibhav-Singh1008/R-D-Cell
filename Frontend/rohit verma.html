Biography

Rohit Verma brings over 11 years of advanced research experience, with a strong foundation in interdisciplinary work bridging artificial intelligence and chemical engineering. His work has emphasized the development of secure, resilient computational models, which can be applied in chemical process optimization, predictive modeling, and smart manufacturing systems.

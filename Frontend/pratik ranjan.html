Biography

Dr. Pratik Ranjan serves as an Assistant Professor and Head of the Department of Computer Science. With over 8 years of teaching experience, he is a recognized expert in Cryptography and Blockchain. He was recently honored as the "Best Performing Reviewer for 2025" by the International Journal on Smart Sensing and Intelligent Systems (Web of Science/Scopus) and holds a lifetime membership in the Cryptology Research Society of India.
